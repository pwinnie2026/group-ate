Nellie Nguyen, Kiana Tran, Paige Wu:

Thank you for visiting our "Mapplication"!

Hello, everyone! Welcome to our site, where you can see the connection between Bruin commuters and campus options to reheat and store food! By providing meal-prepping commuters with a platform to comment and share concerns of food storage and reheating appliances, students will have a collective voice that can highlight shared issues that are otherwise neglected by UCLA’s main platforms.
